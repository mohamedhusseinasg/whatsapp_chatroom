FAQ

When is it convenient to use META (Facebook)?
1) If your company is big or medium-sized and you have the resources to maintain a specialist.
2) If your company is small and you are willing to invest time in learning on your own and adhering to strict rules.
3) If you need to do a marketing campaign to WhatsApp numbers that have not messaged you before.
4) If your marketing will be intensive, about 2,000 messages per day.
Considerations for a Marketing Campaign
1) If you use META, it will be secure; it's META's business. On the downside, if a number doesn't exist on WhatsApp it's challenging to know.
2) If you use apichat.io, it is recommended only to send to WhatsApp numbers with which you have conversed before.
Can my number be blocked if I use apichat.io?
Yes, it is possible, but it will likely be for not taking some minimum measures.
Recommendations:
1) It is very safe if you only respond to incoming messages, i.e., the customer writes to you first.
If there is already an incoming and outgoing message in a chat, you can resume that chat whenever you want; days or weeks can pass without a problem.
2) If you are going to create a new chat, make sure the number exists.
If you send many messages a day to numbers that do not exist, WhatsApp may block you.
3) If you are going to create a new chat, make sure to send a message that is welcomed by your customer.
If you are going to charge a debt, the customer may mark it as SPAM, and if you accumulate several SPAM messages in 1 day, WhatsApp may block you.
Tips for using a new WhatsApp number
* If you have been using your new WhatsApp normally for a few days, you don't need to consider these tips.
1) If the customer writes to you first, and you're only going to respond, you can be very calm.
2) If you are going to write to a customer for the first time with your new number, WhatsApp expects you to do so to Contacts you have saved in your phone.
So, in first few days, start a chat only with customers you have saved in your phone.
3) If you didn't follow usage advices and WhatsApp has blocked you, follow steps in "What shall i do if WhatsApp blocks me?" section.
What shall i do if WhatsApp blocks me?
1) If WhatsApp has blocked you, you can request them to activate your account.
Don't mention that you use any application or apichat.io; just say that you are not doing marketing.
2) If you were doing marketing, it means you were not following the minimum recommendations.
In this case, it is better to use META (Facebook).
